Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

In 2024, Bangladesh ranked 106th out of 133 economies in the Global Innovation Index (GII), which ranks countries based on their innovation capabilities. In 2022, Bangladesh ranked 90th for innovation outputs and 112th for innovation inputs

Sustainability

The UN and its partners in Bangladesh are working to achieve the 17 Sustainable Development Goals, which are a global call to action to end poverty, protect the environment, and ensure peace and prosperity

Infratacture

With Bangladesh on track to graduate from the UN's Least Developed Countries list in 2026, new infrastructure is helping open up the country's domestic and international trade. With 150 bridges opened on a single day in October 202.

Empowerment

Empowerment is the process of giving people the power to make decisions, access resources, and solve problems. In Bangladesh, women's empowerment has improved in recent years, but there are still challenges to overcome.

The Vision of Future

It aims to be at the forefront of achieving Honorable Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina's vision of transforming Bangladesh into a country of the digital economy by 2021 and a knowledge-based economy by 2041. Many of the steps taken to build a digital Bangladesh by 2021 have already been implemented.

Digital Transformation

Over the last two decades, Bangladesh has made significant strides in transforming its digital landscape. Policies supporting internet access, e-governance, and ICT education have helped lay the foundation for a more tech-savvy, digitally inclusive nation.

Green Energy

As of 2024, 459 megawatts are generated from 10 solar power plants in Bangladesh. The largest is the Teesta 200MW Solar Park in Gaibandha, launched in 2023. Bangladesh entered its renewable energy era in 2017 with the launch of a 3MW solar power plant in Sharishabari, Jamalpur.

Model Infratacture

The Strategic Transport Plan 2015–2035 includes the construction of five mass rapid transit systems, two bus rapid transit systems, three ring roads, eight radial roads, six expressways etc. The Padma Multipurpose Bridge (PMB) and the Dhaka mass rapid transit line are also ongoing projects.
